About Us

Since 2016, the TELT Foundation has spearheaded efforts to bring tangible improvements to the lives of communities in remote regions of Vietnam and Hong Kong.

Our mission:
By mobilizing volunteers for specific, actionable initiatives such as constructing schools, preparing meals, etc... for children and local residents, we strive to make a lasting impact where it's needed most

“Volunteers are the only human beings on the face of the earth who reflect this nation’s compassion, unselfish caring, patience, and just plain loving one another.”
